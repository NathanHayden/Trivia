Fun Fact

Melinoë is Zagreus’s sister

In Hades II you play as Melinoë, a witch and Princess of the Underworld who sets out to face Chronos, the Titan of Time. It’s a perfect setup for a roguelike where every “run” feels like rewinding and trying again, which is exactly how this trivia game works too.
